Guarantees and Features

Guarantees


At Meta Insyt, we prioritize accuracy and reliability in the data we deliver. We are proud to offer a 100% guarantee on the accuracy of various information fields, ensuring you have the most precise insights for your business. Our commitment extends to the following key data points:

• Company Name
• Physical Address
• Web Address
• Annual Turnover
• Number of Offices
• Vertical/Industry
• Employee Strength
• Annual IT Budget and Spend
• Contact Full Name
• Job Title of the Contact
• Technology Used
• Contact LinkedIn Handle
• Company LinkedIn Handle
These information fields undergo rigorous research, verification, and regular updates to provide you with the most up-to-date and accurate technographics data available.

Additionally, we provide a 98% guarantee on the accuracy of Email Addresses and Phone Numbers, recognizing the dynamic nature of contact information. While we make every effort to maintain the utmost accuracy, occasional changes or updates may occur.

Should you come across any discrepancies in the guaranteed fields, our dedicated support team is readily available to assist you promptly and rectify any inaccuracies.

With our unwavering commitment to accuracy, including the technologies used, you can confidently rely on our technographics data to drive informed business decisions and gain a competitive edge in your industry.

Features-



1. Comprehensive Insights: Our data encompasses detailed information about the technologies used by companies, giving you valuable insights into their tech stack and infrastructure.

2. Contractual Guarantees: Our guarantees are included in the contract, providing you with legal assurance and transparency regarding the accuracy of the data you receive.

3. Ongoing Data Maintenance: We continuously validate and update our data to reflect changes in the technology landscape, ensuring you have access to the most up-to-date and relevant information.

4. Dedicated Support: In the rare instance of any discrepancies, our dedicated support team is readily available to assist you, addressing and resolving any issues promptly.

5. Unlimited Usage: Once the order is placed and the required database is delivered, the client will own the database with an unlimited usage license.

6. Swift Resolution for Inaccuracies: In case you find any discrepancies or inaccuracies with the database, please let us know at info@meta-insyt.com and we will resolve it at the earliest.
